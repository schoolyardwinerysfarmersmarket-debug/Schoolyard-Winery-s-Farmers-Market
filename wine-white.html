White Wines

Red Wines White Wines Specialty & Seasonal

Spring Break (Cayuga)

This wine tastes like Freedom! Ah those were the days…. Cruising in the convertible, top down, hair in the wind, and sun on your face. If only you had been old enough to drink….sigh! Well, today is your lucky day! Our driest white wine will quench your thirst and put a smile on your face. Don't forget your sunglasses!

Sock Hop (Gewurztraminer)

Break out those saddle shoes and poodle skirts…. it's time to go dancing! Grab the hand of the person next to you and let's put your favorite song on the jukebox. Just one glass of this aromatic, sweet white wine will inspire your inner diva. Notes of Rose will remind you of romance.

Old-School (Dry Riesling)

Steeped in the traditions of your hometown and that all-important Alma Mater, your very persona tells of the contradictions in manners, mayhem, and maturity. However, don't let yourself get caught up in tradition and caught off guard by this leaner, dryer version of an old school Riesling. Forget the glass…. You need a bottle with a straw!

Recess (TBD) — Riesling

Were you the one hanging from the top of the monkey bars? Maybe the swings were your thing? This sweet, innocent taste makes this our most popular white wine! It'll leave you feeling footloose and fancy-free!
