Red Wines

Red Wines White Wines Specialty & Seasonal

Schoolhouse Red (Cab. Sauv.)

Notes of black currant and cherries that will instantly remind you of your first real crush. This dry, full-bodied red wine has curves for days. Now all you have to do is work up the courage to ask her out!

Principal (Cab. Franc)

Turn between being cool and running the proverbial tight ship, the Principal can be found in the office alone at the end of a long, hard school day drinking Cab Franc with tomato soup and grilled cheese! This wine has notes of raspberry, bramble, and bell pepper combined to create a complex wine that honors this mythical man.

Honor Roll (Merlot)

Pull back the curtain, it's time to reveal the names at the top of the list! This soft, lush red wine celebrates your every accomplishment with velvety notes of plum and blueberry. Have a glass (or bottle) and take a bow!

Homecoming (Chambourcin)

If you weren't dating the basketball team captain or the head cheerleader, you likely weren't in the running for king or queen. You may have skipped this one altogether. The strong herbaceous flavors and modest tanning of this crisp red wine are your second chance at the crown!!

Class Clown Red Blend

As we all know there was one in every class. Sitting there making faces and spit balls! They were always causing waves of laughter behind the teacher's back! The class clown made standard testing tolerable with practical jokes and distractions galore. Keep your eyes on the prize, and your butt out of the office with a glass of this racey wine spiked with a healthy dose of good, clean fun!

Sweet Victory (Pinot Noir)

This red wine is sweet and unexpected — like stealing from your parents' liquor cabinet… Only Better! It's light-bodied with a loooong smooth finish. This wine's notes of cherry, raspberry, and hibiscus taste like sweet victory; while the harder-to-detect hints of cloves and mushroom likely merit further investigation. Drink this one chilled to perfection!!
