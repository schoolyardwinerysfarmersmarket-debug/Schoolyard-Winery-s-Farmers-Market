Meet Our Host!!

Welcome Back To the Schoolyard!

Verona's hometown, veteran-owned winery draws on over fifty years of winemaking heritage from Germany's Rheingau region. After a decade of traveling Kentucky's vineyards and perfecting blends, we transformed the historic 1914 Verona High School — once neglected — into a vibrant community hub.

Our venue hosts a lively roster of events: car-cruise-ins, live-music nights, painting and craft classes, book clubs, tribal belly-dance sessions, bourbon tastings, holiday and costume parties, and a Woodstock-themed celebration. From spring through fall, we run a weekly open-air market every Saturday morning.

We also welcome private parties, weddings, and special gatherings. Join the fun at the Schoolyard, located in Verona, Kentucky — just past the Florence Mall and about a thirty-minute drive from Florence.
