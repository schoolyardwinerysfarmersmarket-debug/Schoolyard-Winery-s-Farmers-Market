Welcome to Verona, Kentucky's hometown open-air market — hosted by Schoolyard Winery. Click here to learn more about the winery we call home.

We provide a fun, relaxed setting where local youth entrepreneurs, farmers, and artisans share their best products with the community. Join us most Saturday mornings from spring through fall, and attend our evening market every third Saturday of the month for those who can't make the early sessions.

Our offerings change throughout the year, so check back often for all your favorites.

We are always accepting vendors including youth, handmade, homemade, farm made, farm grown, and more.
