Specialty Wine & Seasonal Favorites

Red Wines White Wines Specialty & Seasonal

Fire Drill (Jalapeño Wine)

I smell smoke; Better pull that handle and run!!! This jalapeño wine is the perfect spicy accompaniment to tomato juice and bad behavior. Stir in a little Worcestershire sauce for umami and lime juice for an additional bite. Now you have the quintessential brunch cocktail or hair-of-the-dog hangover cure!

First Kiss (Blueberry)

Do remember your first kiss? Sweaty palms, butterflies in your stomach preceded that first kiss, but it couldn't possibly have been this sweet! This medium-bodied red blend is infused with chocolate to put the experience right over the top. Perfect for Valentine's Day, Sweetest Day, or just the anniversary of the kids' themselves. This chocolate wine is a dessert in a bottle.

Art Teacher

A delicious spiced apple straight from the heart of Verona! Made with locally grown organic apples.

Christmas Break (Spiced)

This spiced red wine smells like Christmas in a bottle! With its hints of cloves, cinnamon, and star anise, this Schoolyard version of an old-world German Glühwein is made to be enjoyed while guests roast chestnuts and sit around a crackling fire. Warm the whole bottle with a few slices of oranges and a cinnamon stick.
